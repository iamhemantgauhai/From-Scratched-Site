Prices of glass replacement may vary depending on the situations such as type of glass, job complications and timings.

It depends if just the glass can be re-glazed by simply replacing the pane. Typically our glazier assesses the damage and glass size. Prices may be higher for emergency
repair.

Replacing glass will always be cheaper as it's a simple fix. However, the window replacement is a long-term fix. Cost factor will always be higher than replacing just the
glass.

The size of the door or window determines the answer. A door takes about 60 minutes to install on average, and an accessible aluminium window takes about 45
minutes. We'll need someone to be there during the project because we usually glaze from the inside.

When you use the customized glass there are huge changes that we would not be able to replace the glass right away. Our first priority at that time is to secure the
